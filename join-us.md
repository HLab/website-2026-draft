            <h2>Graduate Positions</h2>
            <p>Admission to the graduate program is <a href="https://pbs.jhu.edu/graduate/admissions">administered through the Department of Psychological &amp; Brain Sciences</a>. Applications are accepted annually with a deadline in December and recruitment decisions are typically made by March.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="position-card">
            <h2>Undergraduate Positions</h2>
            <p>Undergraduates interested in gaining research experience may apply at any time for volunteer research assistant (RA) positions. Roles in the lab depend on skills and experience, but may include: participant recruitment, running behavioural and neuroimaging experiments, performing literature searches, presenting in lab meetings, and analyzing data.</p>

            <p>If you are interested, please send us:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>your CV, emphasizing research experience and technical skills</li>
                <li>a brief statement of why you are interested</li>
                <li>your academic transcript (a screenshot is fine)</li>
                <li>your weekly availability</li>
            </ul>

            <p>In general, undergraduate RAs are expected to contribute at least 6 hours per week to the lab, and preference will be given to applicants who intend to develop research skills over a longer period.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
